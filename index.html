How we use AI (no chatbot)

Semantic Search Embeddings over titles, abstracts, and extracted facts.
Evidence Cards Section-aware summaries with sentence-level citations.
Consensus Meter Agreement/disagreement across studies for each (organism, condition, endpoint) triple.
Gap Map Coverage matrix to reveal under-studied combinations and propose next studies.
